About

Born and raised in Potsdam, Germany.  After receiving my Abitur in 2009, i was trained as an industrial clerk, which I completed in 2013. I then moved to Berlin, where I worked in the energy sector until 2015, performing various day-to-day tasks using SAP IS-U.
Also in 2015, I began studying the bachelor's degree programme in business informatics at the HTW Berlin, which I completed in 2018. I started the master's programme in business informatics seamlessly and graduated in June 2021 with the profile of a business intelligence specialist.
